About GDG SAGE University Indore

Who We Are

Google Developer Groups (GDG) SAGE University Indore is a community of developers, learners, and tech enthusiasts passionate about building with technology.

Our Mission

  • Build a thriving developer ecosystem.
  • Empower students with industry-ready skills.
  • Encourage collaboration between youth, startups, and industry leaders.

What is GDG?

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are local communities of developers, designers, product builders, students, and tech enthusiasts. They host free and open events to share knowledge, build projects, and create a supportive ecosystem for innovation.

Our Mission

  • Learn & Share: Boost skills through workshops and hands-on sessions.
  • Community Building: Collaborate and mentor within the local tech ecosystem.
  • Build & Ship: Take ideas from concept to prototype through hackathons and sprints.
  • Inclusivity: A welcoming environment for everyone — beginners, students, professionals, and hobbyists.

A Brief History

GDGs started as part of Google’s developer outreach program. Over time, independent local chapters formed worldwide. Today, thousands of chapters exist, all run by volunteers, empowering developers to grow and collaborate in their local communities.

Why Join GDG?

  • Free workshops (Android, Web, Cloud, ML, Flutter, Firebase)
  • Mentorship from industry experts
  • Hands-on project opportunities & hackathons
  • Networking with recruiters, startups, and peers
  • Certificates and badges for select programs

Who Can Join?

  • Students seeking internships and practical experience
  • Professionals aiming to upskill
  • Designers & Product Builders who want to collaborate on prototypes
  • Beginners looking for a friendly space to start learning

GDG — Where Developers & Ideas Grow Together

A community driven by curiosity — workshops, hackathons, mentorship, and real-world projects. Open for everyone.
